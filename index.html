Note: Paste your team data. The tool will automatically extract amounts.

Revenue Conversion Formula

Convert dollar amounts to local currency:

Raw Revenue = (Dollar Amount) × (Percentage ÷ 100) × Exchange Rate

⚖️ Tier-Based Warmup Payment Method

Deduct fixed amount from each mailer based on total dollar revenue tier, then split between warmup people.

Tier-Based Deduction Rules

Total Dollar Revenue Deduction per Mailer Warmup Payment (Each)

$100,000 - $150,000 300 dhs (mailers × 300) ÷ 2
$150,000 - $200,000 500 dhs (mailers × 500) ÷ 2
More than $200,000 750 dhs (mailers × 750) ÷ 2
